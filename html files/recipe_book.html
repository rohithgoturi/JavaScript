Chocolate Chip Cookies

A classic and delcious chip and cookie recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup gramulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees c)
2. Cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
3. Beat in the vanilla extract and eggs until well blended
4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt.
5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.
6. Stir in the chocolate chips
7. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto ungrassed cookies sheet.
